    (Co)geodesic Flow

    (Co)geodesic Flow

    Methods for (co)geodesic flow. These are temporary, until Manifolds.jl provides the same functionality.

    ManifoldHMC.cogeodesic_flow! — Function.
      cogeodesic_flow!(M, x, p, x₀, p₀, t)

    Follow the geodesic with initial point $x_0 \in M$ and momentum $p_0 \in T_{x_0}^* M$ for time $t$, dragging $p_0$ along. The resulting point and cotangent vector are stored in $x$ and $p$, respectively.
